比较简单，就是将日历年和财务年组合成一张表，打不同的标签

假设存在两张表，日历年和财务年

合并两张表````

```js
FY & CalTable = 
UNION(
    SELECTCOLUMNS(
        'Calendar',
        "Date", 'Calendar'[Date],
        "Year", 'Calendar'[Year],
        "Month", "CY " & 'Calendar'[Month],
        "Label", "By CalYear",
        "Sort", 'Calendar'[Index] * 1
    ),
    SELECTCOLUMNS(
        'F Calendar',
        "Date", 'F Calendar'[Date],
        "Year", 'F Calendar'[FY],
        "Month", "FY " & 'F Calendar'[Month],
        "Label", "By FisYear",
        "Sort", 'F Calendar'[Index] * 13
    )
)
```
